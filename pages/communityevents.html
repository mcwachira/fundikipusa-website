fundi kipusa farming 4.0

Every year we hold community events where we partner with various organizations in the communities to and provide clothes , groceries, meals and health services. This is an event is an act of kindness through which we help families in need. Its is also through events like this that students who have gone through our education program gt to give back to the communities in form of volunteering , cooking and providing hot nutritious meals, and providing clothes and other essentials items needed by the families.

WHY A FOOD PROGRAMME ?

Communities are the life line of nations and fundi kipusa is committed to partner with volunteers, businesses , churches and organizations tha believe in empowering communities through kindness and generosity.

Our goal of providing meals to to needy families has enabled many families find it easy to put food on the table for their Children.This in turn has helped change the communities for the better

Partnering with community leaders hs helped us identify the most vulnerable families enabling us to provide them with at least a meal a day and regular heath checkups and this has helped strengthen the communities more.

Studies in kenya in the recent past has shown that agricultural education programs and modern farming practices has helped increase their yields.

our approach

Nutritious meal

We provide nutritious meal and monitor the how the children in our feeding program are progressing. We also implement sustainability programs like garden and container farms and educate the parents of this children on how to gow and provide nutritious food easily

clean & safe water

Clean water is life sustaining. That’s why Convoy of Hope has committed to the collection of water and the distribution of water filtration systems. We also train and equip local partners where safe water is not available.

safe living conditions

Dangerous structures and improper sanitation can derail a child’s life. We help communities create healthy living environments by teaching appropriate sanitation practices and showing locals how to build proper latrines.
