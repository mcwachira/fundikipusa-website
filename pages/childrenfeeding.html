fundi kipusa farming 4.0

Through providing meals for needy children we have also been able to also provide clean water and educate their families on how to do modern farming and grow food that will sustain them. We have provided food for more than 5000 children in our program and currently we have more than 10 communities active in the program.

WHY A FOOD PROGRAMME ?

The goal of our feeding program has been to promote health and hunger and poverty free children and communities. Most of the children in our feeding program have only one meal and through fundi kipusa and our partners we have been able to provide for them a nutritious meal and clean water.

We believe that children are essential members of their communities who can, one day, become leaders who bring positive change to their countries by breaking the cycle of poverty and hunger. To accomplish our goal, we use the daily meal as a starting point to build strong, loving communities that have clean water, healthy living environments, education, and livelihood opportunities.

A health meal provides a door way for a child to have a better future and escaping the cycle of poverty.

our approach

Nutritious meal

We provide nutritious meal and monitor the how the children in our feeding program are progressing. We also implement sustainability programs like garden and container farms and educate the parents of this children on how to gow and provide nutritious food easily

clean & safe water

Clean water is life sustaining. That’s why Convoy of Hope has committed to the collection of water and the distribution of water filtration systems. We also train and equip local partners where safe water is not available.

safe living conditions

Dangerous structures and improper sanitation can derail a child’s life. We help communities create healthy living environments by teaching appropriate sanitation practices and showing locals how to build proper latrines.
